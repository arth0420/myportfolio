Courses We offers

Navrachana University in Vadodara, Gujarat, offers a diverse range of courses aimed at fostering holistic education and professional development. Their programs span various disciplines, including engineering, business administration, law, liberal studies, and more. Each course is designed to provide students with both theoretical knowledge and practical skills, preparing them for successful careers in their chosen fields. The university's curriculum is regularly updated to reflect current industry trends and advancements, ensuring that students receive a relevant and high-quality education.

Intermediate

Navrachana University offers a wide range of courses across various disciplines. The undergraduate programs include Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) with specializations in Computer Science & Engineering, Civil Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, and Electrical & Electronics Engineering. The university also offers Bachelor of Computer Application (BCA), Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA), and Bachelor of Science (B.Sc) with specializations in Data Science, Biomedical Science, Chemistry, Life Sciences, and Microbiology. Additionally, there are programs like Bachelor of Design (B.Des) in Interior Design, Product Design & Visual Communication, and Bachelor of Arts (BA) in Journalism and Mass Communication, Humanities and Social Sciences. For those interested in teaching, there is a Bachelor of Education (B.Ed) program. At the postgraduate level, Navrachana University offers Master of Business Administration (MBA), Master of Technology (M.Tech) with specializations in Structural Engineering and Computer Science & Engineering, and Master of Science (M.Sc) in Chemistry, Life Sciences, Microbiology, Biotechnology, and Clinical Embryology. The university also provides a Master of Laws (LLM) and various Doctor of Philosophy (Ph.D.) programs in disciplines such as Business Studies, Science, Liberal Studies, and Engineering. Other notable programs include the Executive MBA and Bachelor of Architecture (B.Arch).

Our Beautiful Campus

About Us
